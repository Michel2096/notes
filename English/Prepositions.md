## Las 4 trampas más comunes de esta sección:

```
1. written with her ❌ → written BY her** ✓ (BY = agente, WITH = instrumento)  
2. different of ❌ → different FROM ✓ (expresión fija)  
3. afraid from dogs ❌ → afraid OF dogs ✓ (fija con OF)  
4. made from gold ❌ → made OF gold ✓ (material permanente visible)
```
